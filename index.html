About Modon

Modon Holding PSC

Modon Holding PSC is one of the UAE's most distinguished development companies, headquartered in Abu Dhabi. The Group develops, operates, and manages a diverse portfolio spanning residential, commercial, and mixed-use communities across the region.

With a relentless focus on quality, sustainability, and innovation, Modon has established itself as a benchmark developer — delivering master-planned communities and iconic landmarks that define the skylines of tomorrow.

In 2025, Modon delivered outstanding growth — reporting AED 13.8 billion in full-year revenue and AED 3.9 billion in net profit, underpinned by an AED 46.0 billion revenue backlog. This reflects an accelerated execution of its strategy and a robust, scalable operating model.
